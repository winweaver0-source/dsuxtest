Competition Rules

To participate in this monthly leaderboard competition, you must use referral code "demisux" when signing up on your stake account. Wager counting is based on game RTP: games with RTP of 98% or lower count 100% of wagered amount, games with RTP above 98% count 50% of wagered amount, and games with RTP of 99% or higher count 10% of wagered amount. Leaderboard updates every 5 minutes.
